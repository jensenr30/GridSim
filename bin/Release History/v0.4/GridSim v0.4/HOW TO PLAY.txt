--------------------------------------------------------------
	CONTROLS
--------------------------------------------------------------
WASD
		move character around the map

Left click
		Gather material
Right click
		Place material

Scroll up/down
		Zoom in/out
	
Space Bar
		Pause

C
		Clear the entire grid
	
F1
		world generation (in development)

F3
		debug screen
